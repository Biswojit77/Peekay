Steps:
1. Open js/config.js and replace PASTE_YOUR_WEB_APP_URL_HERE with your Apps Script /exec URL.
2. In Google Sheet create tabs:
   - Registration
   - Feedback
3. Paste apps-script-code.txt into Apps Script.
4. Deploy as Web App:
   Execute as: Me
   Who has access: Anyone
5. Copy /exec URL and paste in js/config.js.
6. Open index.html in browser and test register + feedback.
7. In admin.html replace PASTE_YOUR_GOOGLE_SHEET_LINK_HERE with your Google Sheet link.
